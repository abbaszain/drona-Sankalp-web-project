Discipline

  • General Discipline - Resonance bears the responsibility of its students' academic performance and discipline within its premises only and is not responsible for the students' general behavior , act & conduct, boarding & lodging arrangements and discipline outside Resonance premises. Parents are advised to remain in touch with their wards and keep a strict vigilance on his/her activities and behavior throughout the academic session.

  • Identity Card - On successful registration of students at Resonance, they get the identity of a "RESOnite" in the form of an I-Card. Students are not allowed to enter Resonance premises if they are not wearing their I- Card. A student's I-card also enables access to Resonance's academic resources. Detailed guidelines related to the I-card are provided in Reso Rule Book.

  • Student Uniform* - Resonance provides two pairs of uniforms (T-Shirts) for its students at the Kota Study Centre and it is mandatory for the students to wear their uniforms while attending classes or tests.

  • Attendance System - At Resonance, students' attendance in classroom sessions and tests is vital to their success in JEE(Main+Advanced), JEE(Main) and NEET(AIPMT) and we have a regular attendance marking and monitoring system. The students/parents are informed of students' attendance regularly.

  • Vacations - There is no long vacation at Resonance during course duration except for 9 days during Diwali. Resonance observes National Holidays and is also closed on its Foundation Day (11th April), its Felicitation Function - VICTORY and on Holi / Dhulandi.
